ABOUT US

Hair Day is based in Madison, Wisconsin, Hair Day is a boutique hair salon that specializes in cut, color and styling. Hair Day also offers makeup and nail services, prides itself on its warm and relaxing atmosphere and is best known for edgy hair colors and on-trend cuts.

The salon can only accommodate a small number of coloring appointments each week, so clients need to book far in advance. To keep clients inspired between appointments, we have uploaded makeup and nail tutorials right on our website. Enjoy!

Meet the Owners of Hairday's Salon

Sophia Reed - A master stylist with over 15 years of experience, Sophia is a true hair artist known for her precision cuts and creative coloring techniques. She started her career in top salons across New York City, honing her skills before co-founding Hairday's Salon. Sophia believes every hairstyle tells a story, and she's passionate about helping clients express themselves through their hair.

Ethan Cole - A hair care expert and certified trichologist, Ethan brings a deep understanding of scalp health and personalized hair treatments to Hairday's Salon. With a background in cosmetology and a flair for contemporary styling, he ensures every client not only looks good but feels great about their hair. Ethan is dedicated to creating a welcoming space where beauty meets confidence.
